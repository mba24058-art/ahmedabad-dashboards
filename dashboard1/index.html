Context Analysis: The Ahmedabad Advantage

Ahmedabad's Economic DNA

Ahmedabad's economy is diversified, with a strong services sector, including IT and finance, complemented by robust manufacturing. This provides a stable foundation to absorb and leverage Olympic investments, particularly through the GIFT City financial hub.

Lessons from Past Olympic Hosts

Analysis of past Games reveals a clear lesson: legacy is not automatic. While Barcelona achieved sustained GDP growth, others faced underutilized infrastructure. Our model is designed to mirror Barcelona's success by integrating post-Games utility into every stage of planning.

Impact & Measurement: Projecting the Legacy

Projected Job Creation by Sector

The Trident model drives job creation across multiple phases. An initial surge in construction is followed by sustained growth in high-value sectors like hospitality, technology, and financial services, ensuring long-term employment stability.

The Olympic investment, channeled through our model, is projected to create a significant 'Legacy Bump,' elevating the state's Gross State Domestic Product (GSDP) trajectory well above its current trendline for a decade post-Games.
