De-Risked Financial Architecture

Our financial model is built to attract significant private capital while minimizing public exposure. A key innovation is the introduction of "Green Legacy Bonds," a novel financial instrument to fund sustainable infrastructure, offering attractive returns and allowing global investors to participate directly in India's green growth story.

  • ● Private Sector / PPP (50%): Capital for revenue-generating assets, de-risked via long-term operating contracts and asset conversion plans managed by A-LOCC.
  • ● Public Sector (35%): Strategic government funding for essential, non-commercial infrastructure (e.g., security, primary transit arteries).
  • ● Olympic Revenues (15%): IOC funds, media rights, and sponsorships.
